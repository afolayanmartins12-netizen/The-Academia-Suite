# 🎓 Project Blueprint: Offline Academia Suite
**Project Goal:** To create a data-efficient, high-performance student assistant that functions entirely offline, focusing on massive-scale attendance, local AI, and academic management.

---

## 🏗 Pillar 1: High-Speed "Pulse" Attendance
**Objective:** Mark 1,000+ students simultaneously without internet or manual codes.

*   **Logic:** **BLE Beacon Broadcasting.**
    *   **Lecturer Role:** Acts as a "Peripheral" (Broadcaster). One tap sends out a unique Service UUID.
    *   **Student Role:** Acts as a "Central" (Listener). The app uses **Passive Scanning** to detect the lecturer’s signal.
*   **The "Verified Course Relay":** 
    *   To cover large halls, the app uses a mesh-lite relay.
    *   **Constraint:** A student's phone will only "re-broadcast" the signal to help those further back if that student is **registered for that specific course** in their local database.
*   **Security:**
    *   **Hardware Lock:** One account per **Device ID** (UUID/IMEI).
    *   **Biometric Anchor:** Fingerprint scan required to activate the "Mark Attendance" button once the signal is detected.
    *   **Proximity Check:** Uses RSSI (Signal Strength) to ensure the student is actually in the room, not in the parking lot.

---

## 🤖 Pillar 2: The "AI Brain" (Edge AI)
**Objective:** Provide a smart tutor that costs zero data to use.

*   **Implementation:** **MLC LLM SDK** integrated into React Native.
*   **Model:** 4-bit quantized **Phi-3-mini** or **Gemma-2B** (stored locally, ~2GB).
*   **Features:**
    *   **Contextual Summarizer:** Summarizes downloaded PDFs or pasted notes.
    *   **Quiz Generator:** Creates flashcards and MCQs based on specific course materials.
    *   **Formula Assistant:** Explains mathematical or CS logic offline.

---

## 📂 Pillar 3: Push-to-Offline Document System
**Objective:** Ensure students have materials even if they miss a group chat link.

*   **Flow:** 
    1.  **Onboarding:** Student selects **Department** and **Level** (e.g., CS, 300L).
    2.  **Notification:** Lecturer uploads a PDF; a **Silent Firebase Cloud Message (FCM)** is sent to all 300L CS students.
    3.  **Auto-Sync:** The app wakes up in the background and downloads the file whenever the student has a brief internet connection.
*   **Storage:** Categorized by Course Code in an in-app **Document Vault**.

---

## 📊 Pillar 4: Utility & Lifestyle Suite
**Objective:** Replace multiple apps with one integrated dashboard.

*   **CGPA Engine:** Advanced calculator with "What-If" mode and "Target Grade" forecasting.
*   **Attendance Tracker:** Visual alerts (e.g., "72% - Warning: You are near the 70% limit").
*   **Financial Tracker:** Offline logger for daily expenses (Food, Transport, Handouts) with budget alerts.
*   **Dynamic Timetable:** Local alarms for lectures and automatic "Focus Mode" triggers.
